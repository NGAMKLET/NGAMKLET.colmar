Delhi Public School R.K. Puram is one of the premier educational institutions in India providing the nation with the best of engineers, doctors, bureaucrats, advocates, economists and above all, responsible citizens. An annual feature is the Scholar Badge Ceremony, which is held for every class. At this ceremony, the academic and other achievements of our students are rewarded. The academic challenges and opportunities offered by the school inspire them to attain coveted positions in India and overseas. A student is adjudged a scholar from Class V (Final exam score). The very first time that they are eligible for a blazer is at the end of Class VII for 3 successive years of excellence in academics.

We also honour students from other DPS Schools, who have won Scholar Badge/Blazer and term it as continuous assessment, when considering the criteria for Blazer/Tie/Gold Medal etc. A commendation certificate is also awarded to students who show 15% improvement in their overall aggregate in the Final Examination. Students who have shown merit in Sports, Music, Information Technology, Art or Dance are awarded Red badges, Blazers, Ties and Gold Medals.

Green Badge Given in the first or second instances of Excellence in Academics
Blue Blazer Excellence in Academics for 3 successive years
Blue Badge Given to Blazer Holders in 4th and 5th Year for Excellence in Academics
Blue Tie Excellence in Academics for 6 successive years
Gold Medal Excellence in Academics for 7 successive years
